What is ĪKṢAṆA?

IKSANA is a mobile learning application designed to assist nurses and nursing students in their education and training. Through the use of virtual reality (VR), simulations, and live VR interactions, IKSANA offers a unique and innovative approach to learning. This technology has the potential to not only provide great help to individual learners but also to train large groups of people with minimal human effort.

How Does ĪKṢAṆA work?

By leveraging the power of new-tech like AI, ML & VR, ĪKṢAṆA can create immersive and engaging simulations that enable nursing students to practice a wide range of scenarios in a safe and controlled environment. This can help them develop critical skills and knowledge that are essential for their profession. Additionally, the live VR interactions allow students to engage with instructors and peers in real-time, creating a collaborative and interactive learning experience. Overall, ĪKṢAṆA has the potential to revolutionize the way nurses and nursing students learn and train. By providing a more effective and efficient learning experience, this technology can ultimately improve the quality of patient care and contribute to better health outcomes.

Military Nurses

In military nursing training, VR simulations can be used to train nurses in a variety of scenarios that they may encounter in the field. For example, they can practice administering medical care to wounded soldiers in a combat zone or learn how to operate medical equipment in high-pressure situations. With ĪKṢAṆA's VR technology, military nurses can gain valuable experience and skills that can prepare them for real-world situations.

Emergency Medical Services

VR can be used to simulate various medical emergencies and train EMS personnel on how to respond appropriately. For instance, paramedics can practice performing emergency procedures, such as CPR or administering medications, in a controlled environment that closely mimics real-life situations. By using ĪKṢAṆA's VR technology, EMS personnel can gain hands-on experience and develop critical skills that can help them save lives in emergency situations.

Nursing Training

VR can be used to simulate a wide range of medical scenarios, from routine patient care to complex medical procedures. For example, students can practice administering medication, monitoring vital signs, and communicating with patients in a simulated hospital environment. With ĪKṢAṆA's VR technology, nursing students and professionals can gain valuable experience and develop the necessary skills to provide high-quality patient care.
